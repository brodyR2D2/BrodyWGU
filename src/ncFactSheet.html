North Carolina extends across three major physiographic regions of the United States—the Coastal Plain (or tidewater area), the Piedmont, and the Appalachian Mountains. In addition to producing a spectacular landscape, this regional variation has influenced the state’s climate, soils, plant life, and human geography. (https://www.britannica.com/place/North-Carolina-state)

FUN FACTS

  1. https://www.providentmetals.com/north-carolina/nc-facts.html The first successful man-powered flight took place near Kitty Hawk, North Carolina in 1903. The Wright Brothers’ achievement helped make modern air travel the marvel it is today.
  2. The oldest state university in the country is the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. It was founded in 1789.
  3. North Carolina also leads the nation in furniture, tobacco, brick, and textile production.
  4. The first English colony in North America, founded by Sir Walter Raleigh, was located on Roanoke Island off the coast of North Carolina.
  5. Pepsi was invented in New Bern, North Carolina in 1898.
